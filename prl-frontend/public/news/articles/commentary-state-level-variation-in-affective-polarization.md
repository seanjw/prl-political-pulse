Harnessing our massive set of survey interviews and MRP (Mulitilevel Regression and Post-Stratification), we generated state-level estimates of affective polarization. Though affective polarization remains high across the nation, it does vary by approximately 6 percentage points between states. Those states with the highest levels of affective polarization are those states with the most competitive elections.
